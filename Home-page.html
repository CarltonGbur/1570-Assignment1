Welcome

This site is a quick hub for a few of my favorite fashion labels. I have been into fashion and thrifting for some time now and always love to showcase my my love of fashion through my outfits, but it is always fun to do it for school too! I Started off thrifting in Highschool, but have since expanded to many different mediums of shopping for clothes. I shop on online reselling websites for more expensive and sought after items because i will typically buy them second hand. FOr my normal day to day clothes i usually just thrift at different stores around Pittsburgh. My favorite store to shop at in Pittsburgh is a vintage store called Monarch Studios. I have become good friends with teh owner, Sean, and he always has some great stuff in his collection .
